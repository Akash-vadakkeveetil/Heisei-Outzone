Be a volunteer

Help others to survive

Resource Distribution: Distribute essential supplies like food, water, and medicine to those in need, ensuring timely and prioritized delivery.

Data Collection: Gather and report accurate on-the-ground information about needs and conditions to help target aid effectively.

Medical Assistance: Provide basic first aid and support, coordinating with medical professionals for serious cases.

Shelter Management: Set up and maintain temporary shelters, ensuring they are safe, clean, and well-supplied.
